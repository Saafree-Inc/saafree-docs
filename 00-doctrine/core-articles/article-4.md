# ARTICLE 4

## AUDITABILITY, EVIDENCE & CONSTITUTIONAL REVIEW

**Status:** Foundational Governance Article

---

### Article 4.1 – Principle of Full Auditability

All AI Agent actions shall be auditable by design.

No action may be executed without generating sufficient evidence for review.

---

### Article 4.2 – Evidence Requirements

Execution evidence shall include:

* Authority source
* Permission state
* Risk classification
* Context snapshot
* Execution outcome

Evidence shall be tamper-resistant.

---

### Article 4.3 – Immutable Logging

Saafree shall maintain immutable logs for:

* Delegation events
* Permission changes
* Execution decisions
* Override actions

Log integrity is a constitutional requirement.

---

### Article 4.4 – Post-Execution Review

Actions exceeding defined impact thresholds shall be subject to mandatory review.

Review outcomes may include:

* Policy adjustment
* Authority revocation
* System-level safeguards

---

### Article 4.5 – Constitutional Violation Detection

Saafree shall continuously monitor for violations of constitutional rules.

Detected violations shall trigger:

* Immediate mitigation
* Incident recording
* Escalation to human authority

---

### Article 4.6 – Review Authority

Constitutional review may be conducted by:

* Authorized human governance bodies
* Designated compliance systems

AI Agents shall not review their own constitutional compliance.

---

### Article 4.7 – Right to Explanation

Human authorities shall have the right to:

* Inspect decision paths
* Access execution rationale
* Obtain intelligible explanations

Opacity shall not override accountability.

---

### Article 4.8 – Evolution Through Review

Findings from audits and reviews shall inform:

* Constitutional updates
* Policy refinement
* System hardening

Constitutional evolution shall be controlled and documented.

---

---

### Article 4.9 – Audit Trail Standards

All audit evidence must meet constitutional standards:

**Evidence Integrity:**
- Cryptographically signed audit logs
- Immutable blockchain-backed storage
- Tamper-evident chain of custody

**Evidence Completeness:**
- Pre-execution context capture
- Real-time execution monitoring
- Post-execution outcome recording

---

### Article 4.6 – Constitutional Review Process

Constitutional reviews shall follow structured methodology:

**Review Triggers:**
- Automatic review for high-risk actions
- Periodic review for medium-risk actions
- Manual review for policy violations

**Review Authority:**
- Governance councils for constitutional compliance
- Domain owners for domain-specific reviews
- Executive oversight for enterprise impact

---

### Article 4.11 – Evidence Chain Architecture

Audit evidence forms an immutable constitutional chain:

**Primary Evidence Sources:**
- Governance Engine decision logs
- Syscall Router execution traces
- Domain boundary crossing records

**Secondary Evidence:**
- Business process state changes
- Agent lifecycle events
- Platform interaction records

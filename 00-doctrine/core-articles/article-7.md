# ARTICLE 7

## CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT PROCESS

**Status:** Foundational Governance Article

---

### Article 7.1 – Principle of Constitutional Stability

The Saafree Constitution shall not be modified arbitrarily.

Stability is required to ensure predictability, trust, and legal alignment.

---

### Article 7.2 – Authority to Propose Amendments

Amendments may be proposed by:

* Authorized governance councils
* Designated human authorities
* Regulatory mandates

AI Agents shall not propose constitutional amendments.

---

### Article 7.3 – Amendment Review Process

All proposed amendments shall undergo:

* Legal impact assessment
* Technical feasibility review
* Risk and safety evaluation

---

### Article 7.4 – Approval Thresholds

Amendments shall require:

* Explicit human approval
* Multi-stakeholder consent where applicable

Automated self-amendment is prohibited.

---

### Article 7.5 – Versioning and Traceability

All constitutional changes shall:

* Be versioned
* Be time-stamped
* Maintain historical records

No amendment may erase constitutional history.

---

### Article 7.6 – Backward Compatibility

Where possible, amendments shall preserve:

* Backward compatibility
* Predictable behavior for existing agents

Breaking changes require heightened scrutiny.

---

### Article 7.7 – Emergency Amendments

In exceptional circumstances:

* Temporary emergency amendments may be enacted
* Duration and scope must be explicitly limited

Permanent adoption requires full review.

---

### Article 7.8 – Publication and Transparency

All amendments shall be:

* Publicly documented
* Accessible to stakeholders
* Communicated prior to enforcement

---

---

### Article 7.9 – Amendment Implementation Process

Constitutional amendments require systematic implementation:

**Technical Implementation:**
- Version control for constitutional documents
- Runtime system updates for new principles
- Governance engine reconfiguration
- Audit trail updates for new requirements

**Testing Requirements:**
- Constitutional compliance testing
- Backward compatibility validation
- Performance impact assessment
- Security implication review

---

### Article 7.10 – Constitutional Evolution Tracking

All amendments must be tracked in constitutional history:

**Amendment Registry:**
- Amendment ID and version tracking
- Effective date and sunset clauses
- Related capability registry updates
- Implementation completion status

**Evolution Documentation:**
- Rationale for each amendment
- Impact analysis and risk assessment
- Stakeholder consultation records
- Implementation and testing results

---

### Article 7.11 – Emergency Amendment Protocols

For urgent constitutional changes:

**Emergency Criteria:**
- Immediate threats to constitutional integrity
- Critical security vulnerabilities
- Regulatory compliance requirements
- Business continuity risks

**Emergency Process:**
- Accelerated approval workflow
- Temporary implementation with sunset
- Full review within 30 days
- Permanent adoption or rollback

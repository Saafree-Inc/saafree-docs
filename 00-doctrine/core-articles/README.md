## 📜 Article Content Overview

### **Article 0: Relationship Between National Law and Saafree Constitution**
**Supremacy of human law; non-personhood of AI; traceability**
- Supremacy of human law; pre-legal enforcement; jurisdictional alignment
- Non-personhood of AI Agents; mandatory human traceability
- Neutrality, non-sovereignty; future constitutional compatibility

### **Article 1: Human Authority & Delegation of Power**
**Human supremacy; revocable delegation; human-in-the-loop**
- Human supremacy; delegation as revocable grant; scope and boundary
- Non-transferability; human-in-the-loop; emergency override; prohibition of simulated sovereignty

### **Article 2: Authority Levels & Risk Classification**
**Graduated authority; risk classification; authority–risk alignment**
- Authority levels (0–4); risk categories; authority–risk alignment
- Dynamic risk reassessment; prohibition of risk obfuscation

### **Article 3: Capability, Permission & Execution Boundaries**
**Capability as constitutional grant; least-privilege; boundary enforcement**
- Separation of capability and intent; execution boundaries; context-aware permissioning
- Least-privilege; chained execution control; boundary enforcement; capability registry integration

### **Article 4: Auditability, Evidence & Constitutional Review**
**Full auditability; immutable logging; constitutional violation detection**
- Evidence requirements; immutable logging; post-execution review
- Constitutional violation detection; review authority; right to explanation

### **Article 5: Incident, Harm & Escalation Protocol**
**Incident classification; harm categories; escalation levels**
- Definition of incident; classification of harm; escalation levels (1–4)
- Immediate mitigation; human notification; post-incident review; remediation

### **Article 6: Cross-Jurisdiction & Localization**
**Jurisdictional respect; localization; conflict resolution**
- Jurisdiction identification; localized constitutional rules; conflict resolution
- Cross-border data and action control; regulatory adaptability; platform neutrality

### **Article 7: Constitutional Amendment Process**
**Amendment authority; versioning; backward compatibility; emergency amendments**
- Amendment review process; approval thresholds; versioning and traceability
- Backward compatibility; emergency amendments; publication and transparency

### **Article 8: Security as Pillar Doctrine**
**Supreme Security Objectives; Core Security Doctrine; Threat Model; Security veto**
- Five Supreme Security Objectives (Control Sovereignty, Human Supremacy, Containment, No Security by Obscurity, Resilience & Continuity)
- Four Core Security Doctrine principles (Assume Total Transparency, Runtime Authority > Design Authority, Human-in-Command AI-in-Execution, Failure is Inevitable Collapse is Not)
- System-level threat model (6 adversaries); Security has veto over any conflicting doctrine
- Cascade to Technical Constitution (08-security-supremacy, S1–S8) and Security Layer Laws

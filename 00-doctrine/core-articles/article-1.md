# ARTICLE 1

## HUMAN AUTHORITY & DELEGATION OF POWER

**Status:** Foundational Governance Article

---

### Article 1.1 – Principle of Human Supremacy

All authority exercised within the Saafree system originates from human or legally recognized organizational actors.

AI Agents shall never possess inherent authority. Any capacity to act is exclusively derived from explicit human delegation.

---

### Article 1.2 – Definition of Human Authority

Human authority is defined as the legally and ethically legitimate power to:

* Make binding decisions
* Approve or deny actions
* Bear responsibility for outcomes

Such authority may belong to:

* Natural persons
* Legal entities
* Authorized representatives thereof

---

### Article 1.3 – Delegation as a Revocable Grant

Delegation of power to an AI Agent constitutes a **revocable, scoped, and conditional grant**.

No delegation shall be:

* Permanent
* Unlimited
* Irreversible

Saafree shall enforce technical mechanisms to ensure revocability at all times.

---

### Article 1.4 – Scope and Boundary of Delegated Power

Every delegation shall explicitly define:

* Permitted actions
* Prohibited actions
* Operational context
* Risk thresholds
* Approval requirements

Actions outside the defined scope shall be automatically denied or escalated for human review.

---

### Article 1.5 – Non-Transferability of Authority

An AI Agent shall not:

* Re-delegate authority
* Sub-delegate power
* Create new agents with inherited authority

unless such capability is explicitly authorized and governed by Saafree constitutional rules.

---

### Article 1.6 – Human-in-the-Loop Requirements

Saafree shall mandate human oversight for:

* High-risk decisions
* Irreversible actions
* Actions affecting legally protected interests

The level of required human involvement shall be proportional to the risk profile of the action.

---

### Article 1.7 – Traceability and Attribution

All delegated authority shall be:

* Attributable to a specific human or organization
* Logged with immutable records
* Auditable across time and context

No action may be executed without a traceable chain of authority.

---

### Article 1.8 – Emergency Override and Kill Authority

Human authorities shall retain the right to:

* Suspend AI Agents
* Modify delegated scopes
* Terminate execution immediately

Saafree shall provide enforceable emergency override mechanisms.

---

### Article 1.9 – Prohibition of Simulated Sovereignty

AI Agents shall not represent themselves as:

* Autonomous authorities
* Independent decision-makers
* Legal or moral actors

Any simulation of sovereignty or self-derived authority is prohibited.

---

### Article 1.10 – Preservation of Human Accountability

Delegation of execution shall not be construed as delegation of responsibility.

Human or organizational authorities remain fully accountable for all outcomes arising from AI Agent actions.

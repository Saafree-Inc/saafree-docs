# ARTICLE 6

## CROSS-JURISDICTION & LOCALIZATION

**Status:** Foundational Governance Article

---

### Article 6.1 – Principle of Jurisdictional Respect

Saafree shall respect the sovereignty of all applicable legal jurisdictions.

AI behavior shall be governed in alignment with local laws, regulations, and cultural norms.

---

### Article 6.2 – Jurisdiction Identification

Saafree shall determine applicable jurisdiction(s) based on:

* Location of affected individuals
* Location of data origin and processing
* Location of deploying organization

Multiple jurisdictions may apply concurrently.

---

### Article 6.3 – Localization of Constitutional Rules

Saafree shall support localized constitutional rule sets, enabling:

* Jurisdiction-specific restrictions
* Local compliance requirements
* Context-sensitive enforcement

Global defaults shall not override local mandates.

---

### Article 6.4 – Conflict Resolution

Where jurisdictional requirements conflict:

* The stricter rule shall apply by default
* Human legal authority shall be notified

Saafree shall not autonomously arbitrate legal conflicts.

---

### Article 6.5 – Cross-Border Data and Action Control

AI Agents shall not:

* Transfer data across borders
* Execute cross-jurisdictional actions

without explicit authorization and compliance validation.

---

### Article 6.6 – Regulatory Adaptability

Saafree shall be capable of:

* Updating rules in response to regulatory changes
* Supporting emerging AI governance frameworks

Adaptation shall be controlled and auditable.

---

### Article 6.7 – Neutral Infrastructure Principle

Saafree shall remain:

* Jurisdiction-neutral in architecture
* Jurisdiction-aware in execution

---

---

### Article 6.8 – Jurisdictional Compliance Framework

Cross-jurisdiction operations require constitutional compliance:

**Jurisdiction Detection:**
- Geographic location tracking
- Data residency requirements
- Regulatory jurisdiction mapping
- International law compliance

**Compliance Enforcement:**
- Jurisdiction-specific rule sets
- Cross-border data flow controls
- International treaty compliance
- Local regulatory adaptation

---

### Article 6.9 – Platform Neutrality Implementation

Constitutional platform neutrality requires:

**Abstraction Layers:**
- Platform driver standardization
- API normalization frameworks
- Protocol translation services
- Capability mapping across platforms

**Neutrality Enforcement:**
- Platform-agnostic business logic
- Multi-platform orchestration
- Hot-swapping capabilities
- Vendor independence guarantees

---

### Article 6.10 – Localization Architecture

Jurisdiction-specific adaptations must be constitutionally managed:

**Localization Mechanisms:**
- Jurisdiction-aware configuration
- Local law integration frameworks
- Cultural context adaptation
- Language and format localization

**Constitutional Controls:**
- Localization scope limitations
- Core principle preservation
- Global consistency requirements
- Local compliance validation

# Security Layer Law 08 – Human Escalation

**Scope:** Mandatory human intervention pathways; Chairman override; emergency halt (02 §16.4).

**Reference:** Constitution `08-security-supremacy.md` (S1, S7); Constitution X (Human Final Authority); `00-security-constitution.md`; `00-constitution-to-security-layer-mapping.md`.

---

## Principles

1. Every escalation path must lead to a human (operator, Chairman); not automation only.
2. Emergency halt and override per 02 §16.4; Human Escalation is one of the 8 Security Governance Domains.
3. Security incident response and takedown (e.g. Marketplace 24h) require human approval.

---

## Execution Fabric Alignment

- **Control Plane:** Human intervention APIs; emergency kill; override workflows.
- **All Planes:** No component has unconditional authority without a human escalation path.
